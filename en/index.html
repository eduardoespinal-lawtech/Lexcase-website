Made for the work you do

If case records are your day, this is for you.

Lexbell was built for professionals who live between documents, deadlines and clients. The same app fits a law firm, a real estate office or an immigration consultancy — each case type with the right fields.

Judicial

Court of jurisdiction, opposing party, opposing counsel, hearings and next actions — all in one case view.

Administrative

Filings before DGII, DGJP, ministries and other agencies. Responsible officer, administrative case number and reply deadlines.

Immigration

DS-160, DS-260, A-Number, NVC Case, VAC and consular appointments — the full immigration case.

Business

Company formation and corporate filings in DR (SRL, SA, EIRL, SAS) and USA (LLC, C-Corp, S-Corp).

Rental

Contracts under DR Law 85-25 with automatic deposit cap and annual raise alerts.

Real estate sale

Full process from negotiation to Title Registry recording, with liens and encumbrances verification.

Cases by practice area

Complex matters, the right templates.
